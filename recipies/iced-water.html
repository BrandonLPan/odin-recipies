Iced Water

Description

This is the recipe to the most refreshing drink on earth! Drink at 3AM for best results.

Ingredients:

  • Ice
  • Hydrogen
  • 2 Oxygens

Steps

  1. Put water in the ice tray
  2. Put two hydrogen molecules and one oxygen molecule on the crafting table
  3. Combine the ice and the product you just created and pour into a glass
  4. Success!
